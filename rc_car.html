RC Car Cover

A custom-designed replica of Lightning McQueen rc car cover. I used Fusion 360 to design a 3D model of the car cover and had it 3D printed for a friend. The tools used in the design process included sketching, extruding and adjustments on planer surface to sketch harder edges. I also used the mirror feature to create symmetry on the design.
