BCTS

I worked along side the project lead to plan and design a system that would test the coagulation levels in a person's blood. The system would use a microcontroller that would activate a dropper to add thermoplastin to a blood sample and then use a camera to monitor the copper ball that is placed in the blood sample to determine the coagulation levels. The microcontroller would also control a vibration motor under a plated surface were the blood sample is placed. The system would have a display that shows the results of the test. We created a custome PCB for the switch board to give power to the device. We made a rough draft of a Bill of Materials (BOM). I helped 3D model the wall for the switch board and the LCD display as well as the clips for the display to slide into.
